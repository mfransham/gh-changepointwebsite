Bespoke training and facilitation

Clark and Lydia are regularly commissioned by organisations across the public, private and voluntary sectors to develop bespoke training packages, or to facilitate groups and workshops. We are always keen to explore potential collaboration.

Recent commissions include:

  • A training package for Hertfordshire Constabulary to enhance their skills for interviewing suspected sex offenders
  • A training package for the National Offender Management Service to empower Victim Liaison Officers
  • A training package for the Make A Change Team, Suffolk County Council, on attachment based practice, loss and trauma
  • Numerous conference workshops for organisations including the NSPCC and Circles of Support and Accountability
