Research and programme development

Clark and Lydia have extensive experience in writing programmes for use with those convicted of serious violent or sexual offences, in the criminal justice and mental health sectors. Our new book Changing Offending Behaviour will be published in July 2014.

Programmes we have developed include:

  • A voluntary intervention for men who admit interpersonal violence, but have not been convicted, via Hertfordshire Constabulary
  • A full programme for male patients who have committed sexual offences, with responsivity options for personality disorder, learning disability and mental health issues
  • A programme for use with men who deny having committed sexual offences, for use in the Probation Service
